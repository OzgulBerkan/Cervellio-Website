How does Cervellio work?

BRANDS

Cervellio provides eCommerce capabilities or enhance the current eCommerce capabilities of your brand website.

All we require is a product web service and 3 lines of standardized javascript code on your website (a code block just like google analytics). Cervellio takes care of the rest.

Cervellio regularly updates its product database from your services and detect new/updated information. Cervellio’s machine learning based smart algorithms match your products with products on eRetailers, creating direct smart links to eRetailers of your choice.

Cervellio is hassle free!

eRETAILERS

Cervellio provides high intent customer traffic to your site from brand web sites.

All we require is a product catalogue web service. Cervellio takes care of the rest.

Cervellio regularly updates its product database from your services and detect new/updated information. Cervellio’s machine learning based smart algorithms match your products with products on brand websites, creating direct smart links to your products.

Cervellio is a breeze!
